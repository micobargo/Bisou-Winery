Our History

Bisou, a family owned boutique winery, is located in the heart of Napa Valley. Bisou began its journey in 2004 to be recognized as one of the great wine estates in the world. Handcrafting wines from historic vineyards in Napa Valley, Bisou creates a wine that uniquely expresses the essence of the terroir of this famous wine region. Surrounded by vineyards and mountains, the beauty of this rustic, farm-style winery is as captivating as its wines.

The Vintners

World-class leadership

Benjamin Smith

Owner

Benjamin Smith moved to Napa Valley in 2004 to found Bisou. Benjamin began his wine career in 1989 as a grape grower, planting over 600 acres of grapes. In the late 80’s, he began his multifaceted career in the development of hospitality brands before he created the name and the brand for, Bisou.

Kara Kosley

Winemaker

Kara came to California where he spent five years as winemaker for Newton Vineyard before joining Bisou. Kara’s passion, knowledge, innate ability and scientific training puts him in a unique position to take the best grapes of Napa Valley and transform them into wines worthy of world class standing.

Park Kuzuki

Consulting Winemaker

Park brings her extraordinary expertise to both the vineyard and the cellar. Besides consulting for highly regarded wineries around the world, Park continues to produce her own wines at her estates in Bordeaux where she also directs one of the wine industry’s most respected laboratories.
